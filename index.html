About

La Bella Vita is a fictitious restaurant I am creating here as a front-end personal practice. I chose the name in honour of these 3 years I learnt Italian in high-school with my brilliant teacher. It is also a reference to a very odd italian movie I invite you to check out someday.
This page has been created following the work of designer Virgil Pana. Please go look at his work on Dribble. My name is Cecile, and I am front-end developer in London. If you like this page, please take a look at my projects on Github.
